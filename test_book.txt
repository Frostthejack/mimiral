Chapter 1: The Beginning

Once upon a time, in a land of pure logic and structured data, there lived a small application named Mimiral. Mimiral was a reader — not just any reader, but one who could consume books of many formats.

Chapter 2: The Journey

Mimiral set forth on a journey to parse EPUBs, PDFs, and even the mysterious FB2. Along the way, it encountered many challenges: malformed XML, broken encodings, and the dreaded infinite loop.

Chapter 3: The Resolution

Through careful error handling and resilient parsing, Mimiral persevered. It learned to handle every format gracefully, providing a smooth reading experience for all.

Chapter 4: The Features

Mimiral could highlight text, play TTS, sync with Kavita, and organize books into collections. It was truly a modern reader for the digital age.

Chapter 5: The End

And so Mimiral lived happily ever after, reading books and serving its users with distinction. The end.
