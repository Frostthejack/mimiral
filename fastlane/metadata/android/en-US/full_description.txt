Transform your phone into the ultimate e-book library. Mimiral is a modern, beautiful, and feature-rich e-book reader for Android that supports all major formats — EPUB, PDF, MOBI, CBZ, DJVU, FB2, TXT, RTF, DOC, DOCX, and Markdown.

📖 READ YOUR WAY
• Customize fonts, themes, margins, and page-turn animations
• Day/night mode with adjustable color temperature
• Smooth scrolling or paginated reading
• Vertical and horizontal reading directions

🔊 LISTEN WITH TTS
• Built-in text-to-speech with voice selection and speed control
• Sleep timer for bedtime reading
• Skip chapter, paragraph, or sentence
• Background playback with notification controls

📚 MANAGE YOUR LIBRARY
• Automatic library scanning and metadata extraction
• Collections and bookshelves for organizing your library
• Tags, ratings, and custom sorting
• Reading lists: To Read, Reading, Finished
• Powerful search across titles, authors, and tags

📊 TRACK YOUR READING
• Pages read per day, week, and month
• Books completed and reading time tracking
• Reading streak to keep you motivated
• Set daily, weekly, and yearly reading goals
• Export statistics to CSV

☁️ CLOUD SYNC
• Connect to your Kavita or OPDS server
• Access your library from anywhere
• Built-in free OPDS source browser (Gutenberg, Standard Ebooks, and more)
• Sync reading progress across devices

✍️ ANNOTATE & HIGHLIGHT
• Multi-color highlights with notes
• Export highlights and notes
• Bookmark pages with one tap

⚙️ FULLY CUSTOMIZABLE
• Comprehensive settings for reading, TTS, sync, and library
• Gesture customization
• Backup and restore your settings and data
• Import and export library data

🌐 SUPPORTED FORMATS
EPUB • PDF • MOBI • FB2 • DJVU • CBZ/CBR • TXT • RTF • DOC/DOCX • Markdown

PRIVACY-FOCUSED
Mimiral respects your privacy. Your reading data stays on your device and your personal cloud server. No tracking, no ads, no data harvesting.

Free and open-source at https://github.com/Frostthejack/mimiral
